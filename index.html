About me

I was born on the 21 of July In 1964. As a child, I was orphaned and left with no memory of my parents or if they were alive. I was accepted by a man named Goken, who raised me and took on my training as a martial artist.

I grew up to be a quiet and serious man, yes. My main goal is to master my fighting style perfectly. I love to travel and I take my travel and training very seriously.

I would like to teach people the skills I have achieved, but it requires special things, so if you want to be like me you need to acquire one of these things and then you can become like me.

Education

I started learning fighting at my master Gouken's castle, I was beaten at head way too often to remember when exactly.

I fought a lot with my brother Kena, he taught me how to tolerate pain and many tricks. Subsequent experience I gained in street fights.

Experience

Street Fighter

It was my first tournament. When I beat everyone, I was able to fight with the tournament host, Sagat. In that battle, I hit him hard.

Street Fighter Alpha 2

I gained valuable experience fighting with my father's killer Akuma, he let me reveal the secret power of Satsui-no-hado.

Street Fighter Alpha 3

After being brainwashed by Bayson, I became more vigilant and stronger.

Super Street Fighter II Turbo

It was my second tournament, and here I gave the heat to everyone. And I beat the evil Bayson.

Super Street Fighter IV

Some time after the second tournament, and entered a new tournament to further improve his skills, as well as to meet his old friend Ken in a rematch.

Street Fighter V

I ran into Bayson again, after a long epic battle, I finally defeated him forever.

Street Fighter III

I took part in the Third World Warriors Tournament to further advance my abilities. My most memorable battle was with Hugo, who was able to withstand the Shin Shoruken technique and stand up for himself.
